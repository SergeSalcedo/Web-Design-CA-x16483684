But first...What IS League of Legends anyways?

League of Legends is a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena(MOBA) where 3-5 players from each side of their map battle it out to destroy the opposing teams Nexus and achieve victory! Each player controls their own champion to control with their own unique set of abilities. The main map is known as Summoners Rift and is 5 vs 5 players. There are 3 lanes; top, mid, bottom and then a jungle. The game is very complicated at first but once you understand the basics it can get quite addicting! More information on the game on their website!

SK Telecom T1

is a Korean based e-sports team for League of Legends. The team was established in February 2013 during Season 3 which consisted of Faker, bengi, Piglet, PooManDu and Impact.

The team ended up winning the Season 3 World Championships

Then during Season 4 SK Telecom T1 lost versus Samsung Galaxy.

During the Pre-Season 5, the team expanded to a total of 10 players that included: MaRin, Easyhoon, Bang, Wolf and Piccaboo.

The team went on to win another World Championship in Season 5 with Faker, bengi and KkOma(coach) becoming the first to win two championships.

In Season 6, SK Telecom T1 wins their 3rd World Championship. The team also announces that Duke and bengi will be leaving with Blank and Peanut taking their place, Huni has also replaced Duke in the team
